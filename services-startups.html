NPI to Production

Guiding hardware startups through the critical journey from New Product Introduction (NPI) to full-scale production.

Key Areas of Focus:

  • Design for Manufacturability (DFM): Optimizing product designs for efficient and cost-effective manufacturing.
  • Manufacturing Setup: Establishing robust production lines, quality control processes, and operational workflows.
  • Vendor Management: Identifying, vetting, and managing relationships with domestic and international manufacturing partners.
  • Supply Chain Optimization: Building resilient and efficient supply chains for materials and components.
  • Operational Expenditure (OpEx) Planning: Developing realistic cost models and financial plans for production.

We bridge the gap between your innovative prototype and a scalable, profitable business by ensuring your operations are as solid as your product vision.
